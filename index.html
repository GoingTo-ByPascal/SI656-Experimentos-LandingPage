Special care must be taken when transporting certain products: in many countries it is a crime to traffic in artistic goods and their cultural heritage. Never accept packages from a stranger.

Remember that you have your Embassies and Consulates


Remember that you have your Embassies and Consulates If you are affected by an emergency situation abroad, quickly contact your embassy. One of the most frequent setbacks abroad is the loss or theft of documentation and your embassy is here to help you.
